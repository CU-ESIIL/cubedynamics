# Visualization Overview

This section answers: **How do I inspect and communicate cube results?** It covers cube-aware viewers, mapping helpers, and specialized visuals for events and suitability.

You will find:
- Interactive viewers for slices and summaries.
- Mapping helpers for spatial context.
- Specialized visuals such as suitability tubes and vase hulls.

## Visualization tools
- [Cube viewer (v.plot)](cube_viewer.md): interactive cube slicing and summaries.
- [Maps (v.map)](maps.md): quick mapping helpers for spatial context.
- [Suitability tubes](suitability_tubes.md): discover and render 3D suitability trajectories.
