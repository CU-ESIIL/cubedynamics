# Citations

Please cite data providers alongside CubeDynamics methods. Suggested citations:
- **gridMET**: Abatzoglou, J. T. (2013). Development of gridded surface meteorological data for ecological applications. *International Journal of Climatology*, 33(1), 121–131. https://doi.org/10.1002/joc.3413
- **PRISM**: Daly, C., Halbleib, M., Smith, J. I., Gibson, W. P., Doggett, M. K., Taylor, G. H., Curtis, J., & Pasteris, P. P. (2008). Physiographically sensitive mapping of climatological temperature and precipitation across the conterminous United States. *International Journal of Climatology*, 28(15), 2031–2064. https://doi.org/10.1002/joc.1688
- **Sentinel-2 NDVI**: Didan, K. (2015). *MOD13Q1 MODIS/Terra Vegetation Indices (Version 6)*. NASA EOSDIS Land Processes DAAC. https://doi.org/10.5067/MODIS/MOD13Q1.006
- **Landsat 8 (MPC)**: USGS. (2020). *Landsat 8 Collection 2 (Level-2) Science Product User Guide* (LSDS-1618). U.S. Geological Survey and NASA.
- **FIRED**: Balch, J. K., Iglesias, V., Braswell, A., Rossi, M. W., Joseph, M. B., Mahood, A., Arkle, R. S., & Boer, M. M. (2020). FIRED: A global fire event database. *Scientific Data*, 7, 164. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-020-0524-5
