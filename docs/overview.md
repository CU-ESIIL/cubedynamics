# Documentation Overview

This overview maps the major sections of Climate Cube Math. Use it to understand how the pieces fit together before diving into details.

![Documentation map](assets/diagrams/docs_map.png)

## Orientation
Climate Cube Math documents are organized around a grammar of spatiotemporal analysis. Concepts describe the grammar, capabilities show the verbs, datasets ground the cubes, and recipes illustrate end-to-end workflows. Development guides explain how to extend and contribute.
